One of the most important styling in a webpage is its arrangement. This is basically done using padding, margin and borders. What is the difference between them then?

Padding is the space between an elements content and its border.
Margin is the space between an elements container and the surrounding elements or body of the webpage. It is outside the box of the element.
Border is the demarcation between the elements content and its surrounding i.e outside the element.
